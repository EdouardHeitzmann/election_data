This repository republishes and reformats election data obtained from several
third-party sources. The notices below apply to the relevant data, not to the
repository as a whole.

## Harvard Dataverse / FairVote

The Cambridge, Eastpointe, and Portland collections, and the 2009-2021
Minneapolis collections, were derived from:

- Sabrina Laverty, FairVote, "Proportional ranked choice voting CVRs"
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/STVUET
- License: [CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)

The source dataset was cleaned, reformatted, and supplemented with metadata for
this repository.

## City of Minneapolis

The 2025 Minneapolis Board of Estimate and Taxation and Park Board data was
obtained directly from the City of Minneapolis.

The City's
[Open Data Policy](https://www.minneapolismn.gov/government/charter-and-code-of-ordinances/city-policies/open-data-policy/)
defines open data as public information that should be freely available for
use and reuse. The policy does not identify a standard Creative Commons or
SPDX license for these election files and disclaims warranties concerning the
completeness, accuracy, content, and fitness of public datasets.
